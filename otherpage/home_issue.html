Research Process

  Although most Chinese texts are arranged in vertical lines from left to right, there are still many diversified formats on top of this, including multiple characters on one line, characters in pictures, etc.

  Different from the Western language system, it is composed of a small number of symbols. In Chinese, there are as many as tens of thousands of symbols that make up the language. Among them, individual Chinese characters can be re-disassembled and combined to form new Chinese characters, which greatly increases the model. Difficulty of identification
